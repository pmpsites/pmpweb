Blackpool

Blackpool is famous as a visitor destination, but it’s a perception that obscures a much richer identity and a wider range of place benefits. 

Working with a diverse project partnership, we repositioned Blackpool as a location for business investment, career success and a superb quality of life. Our targeted, online marketing campaign delivered measurable success against ambitious, specified KPIs.

Bicester Garden Town is a new, sustainable community near Oxford, requiring an entirely new place proposition for both investors and residents.

Positioning Bicester within the Oxford region knowledge economy, we developed a place brand, data-supported value propositions, and marketing content encompassing business investment, career growth and quality of life benefits.

Central Bedfordshire is a key growth area at the heart of the Oxford-Cambridge region - but it requires recognition and a defined place proposition.

We developed a place proposition and marketing campaign to attract businesses, skilled workers and new residents, combining knowledge economy investment and career opportunities with distinctive, quantified lifestyle benefits.
